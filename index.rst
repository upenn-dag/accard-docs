Welcome to Accard
=================

Accard is a patient respository for academic medical research teams. It aims to
be easy to use, extensible and universal for all medical research applications.
It was developed by the DAG at the University of Pennsylvania, Abramson Cancer
Center to help support leading cancer research teams.
